🎮 Stream Chat Commands Guide
!profile View your online trainer profile link.
!daily Claims your daily login coins and balls reward.
!coins Check your current coin balance in chat.
!catch [type] Throw a ball to catch the wild Pokémon (types: poke, great, ultra, master).
!shop Lists all items and stone/pack bundles you can buy.
!buy [item] [qty] Purchase items from the shop (e.g. !buy ultraball 5).
!buddy [name] Sets your companion buddy shown on your profile card.
!select [name] Selects active combat fighter for battles.
!fight [@user/wild] Challenge another viewer or fight the active wild spawn.
!accept [name] Accept a pending battle challenge.
!use [stone] [name] Evolve stone-based Pokémon (e.g. !use fire_stone Eevee).
!evolve [name] Manually evolve a Pokémon once it reaches at least 10 wins (random form selected if branched).
!fuse [name] Combines duplicate Pokémon to boost base stats (stars ★ level).
!trade [name] Offer a duplicate Pokémon to the trade pool.
!accept @user Mutually confirm trade exchange with target player.
!join tournament Register your team (auto-picks top 6 CP healthy or custom list) to join the active tournament.
!bet [user] [amt] Wager coins on a tournament match contestant during the pre-match betting window.
!attack Attack the active Gigantamax Raid Boss.
